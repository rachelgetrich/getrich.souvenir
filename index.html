FAQ

How long does an order take?

1–2 business days for regular photo magnet orders. Events are scheduled in advance based on your date.

What photo formats can I upload?

JPG, PNG, or HEIC all work. Higher resolution photos print more clearly.

Do you deliver, or is it pickup only?

Both — pickup is available in Cembo, Makati, and we also accommodate delivery for an additional fee depending on location.

How do I pay?

GCash, bank transfer, or cash on pickup. See our Payment page for details.

Can you cater to large events?

Yes — we've done weddings, debuts, and corporate events. Visit our Book an Event page to get started.
